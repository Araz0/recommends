## Web Development Newsletters

- [JavaScript Weekly](https://javascriptweekly.com/) - A newsletter of JavaScript articles, news and cool projects
- [React Status](https://react.statuscode.com/) - A weekly roundup of the latest React and React Native links and tutorials.
- [Team CSS-Tricks](https://css-tricks.com/newsletters/) - A weekly email newsletter with the most interesting news, ideas, fresh content, thoughts, and incredibly mature jokes from Team CSS-Tricks.
- [Console](https://console.dev) - A weekly digest of the best developer tools.
- [Rendezvous with cassidoo](https://www.cassidoo.com/newsletter/) - In this weekly newsletter [Cassidy Williams](https://twitter.com/cassidoo) going to share with you what's new and cool in the world of web development, with content for everyone, from beginners to pros.
- [Frontend Horse](https://frontend.horse/archives) - Alex Trost talks his latest articles, projects, livestreams and more.
- [The CodePen Spark](https://codepen.io/spark) - A weekly newsletter covering this weeks noteworthy Pens from the CodePen community.
- [Smashing Magazine](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/the-smashing-newsletter/) - Every Tuesday they send out an editorial email newsletter with useful tips and techniques on front-end and UX.
- [Landing Letter](https://www.landingletter.com/) - Learn to build converting landing pages with help of descriptive teardowns, articles, resources and much more.
- [Pony Foo Weekly](https://ponyfoo.com/weekly) - Pony Foo Weekly is a newsletter discussing interesting and trending topics around the web platform. It comes out once a week, on thursdays.
- [Lee Robinson](https://leerob.io/newsletter/) - This newsletter provides a behind-the-scenes look into what [Lee Robinson](https://twitter.com/leeerob) (Head of Developer Relations at Vercel) working on and writing about.

## Design Newsletters

- [The DESK Magazine](https://vanschneider.com/blog/) - by [Tobias van Schneider](https://twitter.com/vanschneider)
- [Dribbble Newsletter](https://dribbble.com) - Weekly newsletters about design workshops, career and freelance project opportunities, latest design news and shots and inspiration from the community tailored for you.
- [Screenlane](https://screenlane.com/) - The latest mobile & web UI design inspiration in your inbox every week.

## Techrelated Newsletters

- [hackernewsletter](https://www.hackernewsletter.com/) - Since 2010, they put out a weekly newsletter of the best articles on startups, technology, programming, and more.
- [TLDR](https://tldr.tech/) - TLDR is a daily newsletter with links and TLDRs of the most interesting stories in tech, science, and coding.
